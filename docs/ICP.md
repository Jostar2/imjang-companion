# ICP.md

## Paying customer

The recommended initial paying customer is:

- a small investor operator
- a buyer agent who needs post-visit reports
- a research assistant or partner who supports repeated field visits

## End user vs. payer vs. report recipient

Do not treat these as the same by default.

- `Payer`: team lead, operator, buyer agent, investor
- `Active operator`: the person who runs the visit checklist in the field
- `Report recipient`: final buyer, partner, spouse, investor, or client

This distinction is central to the B2B2C thesis.

## Jobs to be done

- capture field observations before they are lost across chat, gallery, and notes apps
- turn a visit into a structured score and red-flag record
- compare multiple candidate properties with a reproducible frame
- deliver a same-day review packet to a decision-maker

## High-intent signals

The best early customers will usually have at least two of these traits:

- visit multiple properties per week or month
- already maintain a spreadsheet, Notion board, or photo folder for visits
- need to share findings with a client, partner, or spouse
- feel friction in converting raw visit notes into a clean recommendation
- pay for other real-estate workflow tools already

## Anti-ICP

Avoid targeting these first:

- casual listing browsers who are not visiting properties yet
- users who only want listing discovery
- large enterprise brokerages needing permissions, audit trails, and complex collaboration
- users expecting full offline sync from day one

## Core activation moment

A user is considered activated when they:

1. create a project
2. add at least two candidate properties
3. complete at least one visit with notes and photos
4. open a comparison or report view

## Expansion path

Once the first user or team is active, expansion should come from:

- more projects per month
- more active operators per team
- shared reports sent to external decision-makers
- branded or reusable report templates

## Research backlog

- interview 10 repeated-use operators before locking pricing
- identify whether buyer agents or small investor teams have shorter sales cycles
- confirm whether report recipients need read-only links, PDF export, or both
